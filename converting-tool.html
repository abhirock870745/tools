Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our document converter

Is this service really free?

Yes, our PDF to Word and Word to PDF converter is completely free to use with no hidden charges. We support our service through advertisements, so you can convert as many files as you need without paying anything.

What happens to my files after conversion?

Your privacy is important to us. All files are automatically and permanently deleted from our servers immediately after conversion. We don't store, access, or share your documents in any way.

Is there a file size limit?

Yes, the maximum file size is 50MB for free users. For larger files, we recommend compressing them before uploading or considering our premium plans for higher limits.

Does the converter preserve formatting?

Our advanced conversion technology preserves most formatting elements including fonts, tables, images, and layout. For best results, use the "Preserve original layout" option and upload high-quality source files.
